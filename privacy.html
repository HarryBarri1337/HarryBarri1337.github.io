Privacy Policy

This page explains what information SkinQuest uses to run accounts, coins, rewards, and manual Steam trades.

1. Account data

SkinQuest may store your account email, user ID, coin balance, level progress, coin history, reward requests, and account status through Supabase Authentication and database services.

2. Steam trade URL

If you save a Steam trade URL, it is used so approved rewards can be sent by manual trade offer. SkinQuest does not need your Steam password, Steam Guard code, or Steam API key.

3. Offerwall providers

Partner offerwall providers may collect information needed to show and verify tasks. Their own privacy policies and rules apply when you open their offers.

4. Reward review data

Admins may view reward requests, trade links, statuses, notes, and coin movements so rewards can be reviewed, sent, completed, rejected, or refunded.

5. Security

Do not send private Steam credentials to anyone. SkinQuest will never ask for your password, Steam Guard code, or API key. If a message asks for those, treat it as unsafe.

6. Contact

For privacy, account, or support questions, contact support@skinquestcs.com. Support requests sent from the site are stored so the team can review and reply when needed.
