These terms explain the basic rules for using SkinQuest during the current reward-platform setup.

1. What SkinQuest is

SkinQuest lets users earn coins through free partner tasks and redeem fixed CS2 rewards when stock is available. SkinQuest does not accept deposits, sell coins, run cases, run roulette, or offer randomized gambling rewards.

2. Account rules

You are responsible for your account activity. Do not create duplicate accounts, use bots, automate offers, abuse VPNs, or provide fake survey answers. Suspicious activity may lead to denied coins, rejected rewards, or account removal.

3. Offerwall completions

Coins are credited only after a partner provider confirms a task. Some offers may be delayed, rejected, limited by country or device, or reversed by the provider. SkinQuest cannot guarantee that every attempted offer will credit.

4. Rewards and stock

Rewards have fixed coin prices and limited stock. Redeem requests are manually reviewed. If a request is rejected, refunded, or cancelled, coins may be returned according to the status shown on your dashboard.

5. Steam trade links

You must provide a valid Steam trade URL to receive approved rewards. SkinQuest only needs your public trade URL. Never share your Steam password, Steam Guard code, Steam API key, or login credentials.
