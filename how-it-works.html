How It Works

SkinQuest is a fixed-reward site: earn coins from verified offers, choose a listed CS2 reward, and receive it through a manual Steam trade after review.

Why do I need a Steam trade URL?

It lets the reward be sent without adding anyone as a friend on Steam. Save it on your dashboard before redeeming.

How long does delivery take?

Rewards are manual at the start, so delivery is not instant. Check your dashboard for request status updates.

Can a request be rejected?

Yes. If something is wrong, the request can be rejected or refunded and the reserved stock is released.

Is this gambling?

No. Rewards are fixed listings with fixed prices. There are no random cases, spins, or betting features.
