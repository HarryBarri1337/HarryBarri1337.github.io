Do I need to deposit?

No. SkinQuest does not accept deposits and coins cannot be purchased.

Are rewards random?

No. Rewards are fixed listings with fixed coin prices. No cases, no roulette, no random drops.

Why did I not receive coins?

Offerwall completions must be confirmed by the provider. Some offers can be delayed, rejected, or limited by country or device.

Can rewards run out?

Yes. Rewards are limited and can go out of stock. Out-of-stock rewards are shown at the bottom of the reward shop.
